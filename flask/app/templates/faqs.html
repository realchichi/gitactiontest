FAQS

  • What do I do if I can't find the plant I'm looking for ?

    If you have some information about the plant, such as its habitat, leaf shape, flower color, or growth habit, try using multiple characteristics to narrow down your search. Sometimes, focusing on secondary features can lead to a successful identification.

    I'm sorry, but the platform currently only allows for the upload of a single photo for plant identification. You can select the most representative image that showcases the plant's features clearly.   • What information do I need to provide to identify a plant ?

    High-quality photos that capture different parts of the plant, such as leaves, flowers, stems, and any distinctive features. Close-up shots can be particularly useful for highlighting specific details.

  • Can I use the website to identify plants from any location ?

    Yes, you can typically use websites or online plant identification tools to identify plants from any location.

  • How can I report errors or inaccuracies in the plant information ?

    Navigate to the website's "Contact Us" page: Look for a link or button labeled "Contact Us" on the website's homepage or in the site's menu/navigation bar. Fill out the contact form or email provided with as much detail as possible about the error or inaccuracy you've encountered.
